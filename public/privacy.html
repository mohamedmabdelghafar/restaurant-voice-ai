Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 24, 2026

1. Introduction

SmartJump.AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Voice Ordering integration with Square POS.

2. Information We Collect

  • Merchant Information: When you connect your Square account, we collect your Merchant ID, Location IDs, and access tokens required to manage orders and menus.
  • Customer Data: We process voice data and phone numbers to create orders. This data is transcribed by our AI partners (Retell AI) and transmitted directly to your Square POS.
  • Order Data: We process order details (items, prices, modifiers) to fulfill requests.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide the voice ordering service.
  • Sync menus from Square to our AI agent.
  • Inject orders into your Square KDS.
  • Improve the accuracy of our AI models.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Square: To execute orders and sync menus.
  • Retell AI: To transcribe voice calls (audio processing).
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) to protect your access tokens and sensitive data. All data transmission occurs over secure HTTPS channels.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at: contact@resturantsolutions.online
